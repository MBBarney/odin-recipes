Perfect Pizza!

Precisely pizza. You'll want to save room in your stomach for seconds and thirds.

Follow these steps exactly. If you deviate, even by a fraction of a snitch, and this recipe turns deadly. Instead of pizza, you'll have poison!

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Candied frog noses
  • Tomato sauce
  • Witch's Best brand cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 l of warm tap water with the wheat flour.
  3. Grease the old pizza pan your mother left you in her will.
  4. Spread the water-and-flour blend on the pan.
  5. Spoon in tomato sauce to taste.
  6. Add cheese in generous amount.
  7. Sprinkle in frog's noses.
  8. Bake for 12 minutes.
  9. Call Dominos just in case this goes bad. (Papa Johns is better, but PJ is a jackass. Dominos FTW!)_
