Ice Cream

Perfect for those hot summer days. Ice cream is an all-time favorite of kids and grown-ups alike.

What's your favorite type of cone?

Ingredients

  • rock salt
  • cream
  • dutch chocolate
  • muscle
  • mix-ins

Instructions

  1. 15 years before you want ice cream, give birth to twin boys.
  2. On ice cream day, make up a slush of rock salt and water. Fill the outer container of your antique ice cream machine.
  3. Fill the inner container with the cream and mix-ins
  4. Freeze solid.
  5. Convince your teenagers that cranking ice cream is fun. Tell them it's a contest or something.
  6. Take the ice cream machine out of the freezer and set it on the counter.
  7. Laugh silently while they exhaust themselves cranking, cranking, cranking.
  8. Scoop and enjoy.
