Lasagna!

Description

Just like Mom used to make. Cheesy goodness!

Serves up with perfect combination of "stays square" and "falls apart on the plate."

You're going to love it. Pack on the pounds like it's nobody's business, because that's exactly what it is: Nobody's Business!

Ingredients

  • Three boxes, granulated eye of newt.
  • One section of Snow White's favorite dress.
  • One kettle of bubble bubble toil and trouble.

Steps:

  1. Go to Luigi's local artisan lasagna-makings shop.
  2. Say bonjourno to Luigi.
  3. Tell Luigi you want half a kilo of pasta, a gallon of tomato sauce, a bucket of ricatta, half a kilo of mozarella, and 100g of his secret spice blend.
  4. Back at home, preheat your oven to 400 degrees F.
  5. Fill a large pot with 2l of water. Soak the pasta until flexible.
  6. Cover the bottom of a cake pan with a single layer of pasta.
  7. Ladle in tomato sauce. Spread to an even thickness of 1 cm.
  8. Cover with another layer of pasta.
  9. Add ricatta, 1 cm thick.
  10. Add a layer of mozarella.
  11. Sprinkle Luigi's secret spice blend in a generous quantity.
  12. Top with a third layer of pasta, a little tomato sauce, and a flourish of mozarella.
  13. Bake for 35 minutes.
  14. Remove from oven at 35 minutes. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before cutting.
  15. Serve hot.
  16. Tell your guests "it's nothing. Just a little something you whipped up from leftovers.
  17. Send a thank-you note to Luigi!
